### PART 1: THE SCIENCE YOU CAN'T IGNORE

**1. You don't manage time, you manage energy.** 
Roy Baumeister's ego-depletion model (1998) showed self-control acts like a muscle — finite and depletable. [Research review PMCID]. If you resist temptation, make 100 micro-decisions, and force focus for 4 hours straight, your later decisions get worse [1](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC3096764/). Updated reviews in 2024 show it's not just "willpower" but how your brain allocates limited resources — fatigue is the signal to stop [3](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/383833997_Self-Control_and_Limited_Willpower_Current_Status_of_Ego_Depletion_Theory_and_Research). The implication: **Time management without energy management is useless.** [5](https://exoplan.io/blog/manage-energy-not-time/)

**2. Your brain is not linear, it's rhythmic. 90 minutes ON, 20 minutes OFF.**
Sleep researcher Nathaniel Kleitman discovered in 1963 the Basic Rest-Activity Cycle (BRAC): your brain pulses every 90-120 minutes from high to low alertness, day and night [8](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Basic_rest%E2%80%93activity_cycle). EEG and cortisol studies confirm this [1](https://www.cannelevate.com.au/article/understanding-ultradian-rhythms-brains-90-minute-cycles-explained/)[3](https://neurosity.co/guides/ultradian-rhythm-90-minute-brain-cycles). If you work past 90 minutes, you switch from parasympathetic to sympathetic "fight or flight" mode, prefrontal cortex shuts down, errors spike [7](https://hbr.org/2010/05/for-real-productivity-less-is). Anders Ericsson found elite violinists *only* practiced in 3x 90-min blocks with breaks [2](https://www.sacbee.com/entertainment/living/article315897279.html).

**3. Brain fog is real and it's attention + fatigue, not laziness.**
Research defines brain fog as impaired sustained attention, processing speed, and working memory [1](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC3617392/). Recent COVID/post-viral studies show the core driver is a loop: objective cognitive load + fatigue = subjective fog [3](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11618112/). Two killers you have daily:
   - **Attention Residue:** Sophie Leroy (2009) proved when you switch tasks, part of your attention stays stuck on the last task, degrading performance for up to 20+ min [1](https://www.scribd.com/document/805072153/Deep-Work-by-Cal-Newport).
   - **Interruption Cost:** Gloria Mark @ UC Irvine found it takes **23 min 15 sec** to regain focus after one interruption [2](https://www.sacbee.com/entertainment/living/article315897279.html).

**4. Deep Work = (Time Spent) x (Intensity of Focus).**
Cal Newport's formula [4](https://insightsintomoney.medium.com/unlock-your-superpower-a-deep-dive-into-cal-newports-deep-work-for-unmatched-focus-in-a-0dca7e9699bf). You cannot do 8 hours of deep work. Biology caps you at **3-4 hours/day max**, in 90-min sprints. Csikszentmihalyi's Flow State only happens when challenge = skill + clear feedback + zero distraction [2](https://www.productivepatty.com/neuroscience-of-deep-work-and-flow-state/).

**Translation for you:** You have unlimited time but limited *quality* hours. Your job is to protect 2-3 gold blocks per day. Everything else is shallow.

### PART 3: THE SYSTEM - VIBE-CODER OS v1.0

#### PILLAR 1: ENERGY MANAGEMENT (Fix biology before productivity)

**Non-negotiables (Do these or nothing else works):**

1.  **Sleep Anchor:** 23:30 - 07:30 (fixed, even weekends). No screens 22:30-23:30. Nova Gorica summer = bright till 21:00, so use blackout curtains. This restores the BRAC cycle.
2.  **Light Anchor:** Within 30 min of waking, outside walk 10-20 min (Soča river path ideal). Sunlight resets circadian + cortisol pulse [6](https://smartkeys.org/ultradian-rhythm-work/).
3.  **Movement Snacks:** Research: physical activity in leisure has strongest recovery effect [1](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC3096764/). You sit coding. Protocol:
    - Every 90 min: 5 min walk + 10 air squats + water
    - Daily: 30 min Zone 2 (fast walk, bike to Italy border and back) - this builds brain endurance against mental fatigue [2](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2025.1616171/full)
    - 3x/week: Strength (pushups, rows, squats). At 26M, this directly impacts testosterone, focus, mood.
4.  **Nutrition / Brain Fog Protocol:**
    - Breakfast: Protein + fat (eggs, yogurt, nuts), not carbs alone. Prevents 10am crash.
    - Hydration: 2.5L/day. Dehydration = working memory drop (your fog).
    - Caffeine: 1-2 cups ONLY before 13:00. After = adenosine debt, worse tomorrow.
    - Alcohol: 0 during build sprints. It fragments BRAC.
5.  **Anti-Fog Reset (When fog hits):** Don't push through. Do 20-min NSDR / Non-Sleep Deep Rest (YouTube: Andrew Huberman NSDR) + cold water face + walk. Research shows fatigue is metabolite accumulation (adenosine, glutamate) — rest clears it, forcing worsens it [2](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2025.1616171/full).

#### PILLAR 2: ATTENTION & THOUGHT MANAGEMENT

You have 3 enemies: switching, open loops, noise.

**1. The 90-20-90 Rule (Your Core Rhythm):**
- WORK: 90 min = 1 Ultradian Sprint
- REST: 20 min = true detachment — no phone, walk, stare at trees, snack. This is when memory consolidates [3](https://neurosity.co/guides/ultradian-rhythm-90-minute-brain-cycles).
- Max 3 sprints / day = 4.5 hours. That's more than most "10 hour" workers do.

**2. Deep Work Ritual (Make focus frictionless):**
- Same desk, same headphones, same playlist (Brain.fm or lofi)
- Phone in other room on grayscale
- One browser window: IDE + one doc. Close everything else with Cold Turkey / Leechblock
- Start ritual: 2 min breathing, write ONE outcome for this sprint: "I will make auth work, not fix CSS"

This addresses Attention Residue [1](https://www.scribd.com/document/805072153/Deep-Work-by-Cal-Newport) — you reduce carry-over.

**3. Thought Dump System (GTD for vibecoders):**
Your brain is for *having* ideas, not holding them. 2 captures:

- **Capture Inbox:** Notion or simple `todo.md`. Every idea, bug, anxiety goes there instantly. Empty daily.
- **Shutdown Ritual (18:30):** Cal Newport. Review tomorrow's 3 MITs (Most Important Tasks), close all tabs, write "what's unfinished" — this stops ruminating at night and resets attention residue.

**4. Priority Filter for Single Vibecoder:**
Use ICE scoring for app ideas:
**Impact (1-10) x Confidence (1-10) x Ease (1-10) / 100**
Do only the top score. You are single with no job = your risk is building 5 apps 10% vs 1 app 100%.

Rule: **One active app in BUILD phase, one in DISTRIBUTE phase. Never 2 builds.**

### PART 4: WHEN YOU GET TIRED / FOGGY (Human Protocol)

**Immediate (0-5 min):**
- STOP. Close eyes. 4-7-8 breath x3.
- Drink 300ml water
- Stand, 20 air squats

**Short (5-20 min):**
- Walk outside without phone — attention restoration theory
- If >14:00, NSDR 15 min
- Do NOT open Twitter/Discord — it adds attention residue

**Decision:**
Ask: Am I in Trough (natural ultradian dip) or Depleted (bad sleep, bad food)?
- Trough -> Do shallow task from your list for 20 min, then retry.
- Depleted -> Stop sprints today. Do life admin, go to gym, early bed. Protect tomorrow's 3 gold hours. Remember: 3 great hours > 10 foggy hours [7](https://hbr.org/2010/05/for-real-productivity-less-is).
