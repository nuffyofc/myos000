The Science

Time chunking + task tracking = attention residue reduction (Leroy 2009) + ultradian rhythm alignment (Kleitman 1963). Tracking per task makes deep work visible.

Time Chunking
Naming a task + preset duration reduces decision fatigue (Baumeister). One chunk = one outcome = less switching cost (23m15s per interruption, Mark).
Category Tracking
Visualizing time by category shows 40/60 build vs distribution balance. What gets measured gets managed. Solo devs over-index build.
